FITT Force™ Pro

Description

The the strongest and lightest garden hose for extensive Garden and Professional use

FITT presents our innovative technology that guarantees the combination of high performance durability with exceptional lightness.

Thanks to highly advanced materials the product is extremely lightweigh and insuring ease of use even in the most extreme situations.

FITT Force™ Pro was a winner of this year’s red dot DESIGN AWARD, the world-renowned design prize.

Main use

  • Professional application
  • Large garden
  • Intensive use
  • Abrasive surface

The hose is flat when stored and takes its classic round shape during use. When the flow stops, its returns to the original flat shape: the water drains and the product is immediately handy, compact and easy to store.

NTS Plus e HD-TECH: the perfect combination of technology and performance

With our patented  NTS PLUS mesh, our hose offers even greater strength and performance, in addition to extreme flexibility, preventing tangles and twists and guaranteeing constant water flow.

HD-TECH technology guarantees high resistance to abrasion and puncture. The use of thermoplastic elastomer *santoprene (Santoprene™ TPV from ExxonMobil®) combined with the ultra-light technology patented by Fitt, makes our hose the most flexible and easy to handle.

*Santoprene and Exxon are registered trademarks of ExxonMobil.

Product limited lifetime guarantee

  • High tenacity fiber reinforcement layer insures pressure > 500 psi.
  • High Durable Technology (HD TECH) cover is abrasion resistant and puncture proof.
  • Ultra-soft inner core layer for superior manageability and high flexibility.
  • No PVC, phthalates or lead – “drinking water safe!”.
  • Double NTS chain mesh technology reinforcement eliminates flow restriction due to kinks and tangles.
  • High manageability and high flexibility in extremely cold temperatures (-4°F).

HOSECADDY: the innovative system for transportability and storage of the hose.

The HOSECADDY is the practical FITT storage and carrying belt, lightweight and robust at the same time. It is also perfect for the most demanding construction sites, simplifying the transport and storage y of the hose.

  2. Convenient handle for easy storage
  3. Lightweight, ultra strong polyester material
